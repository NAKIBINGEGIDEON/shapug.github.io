About Us

Our Mission

KAN Sustainable Health Action for Development (SHAP-UGANDA) Limited is a Not-For-Profit Non-Governmental Organization, founded in 2021 by a group of public health professionals. The organization is committed to providing comprehensive healthcare services to marginalized communities in Uganda.

Our Work

We focus on delivering quality health services through community-driven interventions in the districts of Kamuli, Luuka, Buyende, Kayunga, and Namutumba. We engage in a wide variety of health programs including maternal health, disease prevention, and health education.

Our Health Services

Our key areas of service include:

Maternal, Newborn, and Child Health,Prevention and Control of Communicable Diseases,Non-Communicable Diseases Management,Health Promotion & Education,Environmental and School Health, and Gender and Health Initiatives

Our Target Groups

We serve the following vulnerable groups:

  • Adolescent Girls, Boys, and Young Women(AGYW) , Orphans and Vulnerable Children (OVC),Women, Youth, and Priority Populations, People Living with HIV (PLHIV) and People Living with Disabilities (PLWD)

Our Approach

We work closely with local communities to empower them to access healthcare services and strengthen existing structures for long-term sustainability. Our approach emphasizes capacity building, community participation, and inclusion of all groups.

Vision

A transformed community for better health outcomes.

Mission

To improve accessibility to sustainable quality healthcare through empowering vulnerable communities to embrace healthcare services and strengthening existing structures at different levels.

Objectives / Aims

Strategic Objectives

Strengthen community health systems and health promotion
Build capacity of healthcare service providers
Promote community economic empowerment and gender inclusion

Specific Objectives

To promote the availability and utilization of healthcare services in the community
To support the Government of Uganda in the implementation of the Minimum Primary Health Care Packages
To contribute towards a socially, economically, and politically empowered community
To collaborate with stakeholders towards the provision of quality healthcare services
To contribute towards child protection services in the community
