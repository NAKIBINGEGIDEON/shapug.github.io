About SHAP-Uganda

KAN Sustainable Health Action for Development (SHAP-UGANDA) Limited is an indigenous Not-For-Profit Non-Governmental Organization founded in 2021 by a group of passionate public health professionals.

The organization aims to influence development by providing sustainable, integrated, and quality health care services to marginalized and vulnerable individuals in the community. This is achieved through capacity building, empowerment, community-driven health interventions, and active community participation.

We operate in the districts of Kamuli, Luuka, Buyende, Kayunga, and Namutumba, implementing interventions in areas such as Integrated Reproductive Maternal, Newborn, Child, and Adolescent Health services, communicable and non-communicable disease control, health promotion, community health initiatives, and more.

Target beneficiaries: Adolescent girls, boys and young women (AGYW), Orphans and Vulnerable Children (OVC), Women, Youth, Key and Priority Populations, People living with HIV (PLHIV), and People living with Disabilities (PLWD).
